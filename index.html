Pathfinder Godsrain Prophecy Bingo

A whole god is dying!
Arazni is becoming core!
Grab a bingo card to celebrate.

Paizo is going to announce 10 safe gods, one each Wednesday, on their blog in the form of Godsrain Prophecies.

Safe God Bingo:

As each god is announced to be safe, mark them safe on your bingo card with a circle using an image editor of your choice.

  • Earn 1 point for every 4-in-a-row you get horizontally.
  • Earn 1 point for every 5-in-a-row you get vertically.
  • Earn bonus points for getting a bingo early.

Bonus Points

The first time you earn a point, earn bonus points equal to 10 minus the number of gods announced safe at the time you earned your bingo.

That is, if your first bingo is on week 4, you earn 6 bonus points. (Week 5 is worth 5, week 6 is worth 4, week 7 is worth 3, week 8 is worth 2, and week 9 is worth 1 bonus point.)

Dead God Doubler:

Of course, guessing which god is going to follow in Aroden's footsteps is the most fun part.

Select a god you think will die. They will be marked with an X.

If you guess correctly:

  • Earn 1 point.
  • Then double all of your points.

Good luck!
